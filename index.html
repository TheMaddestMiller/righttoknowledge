A Right to Knowledge

All sorts of literature and media are banned worldwide, and I believe that taking away access to information and art violates a human right, the right to knowledge. Everyone should be able to view what they want to, regardless of the country or administration's policies. In many cases, banning can be a little irrational, or even deliberate ignorance when a government bans something based on fear that there may be dissent among their citizens. So, join my cause. Fight against institutions and governments to take back our right to knowledge!
